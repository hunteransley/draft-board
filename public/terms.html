What This Is

Big Board Lab is a free NFL draft board builder and mock draft simulator. You can rank prospects head-to-head, grade them with trait sliders, and run mock drafts against 32 AI-powered general managers. It's built for fans, by fans.

Your Account & Data

You can sign in with Google to save your work. When you do, we store:

  • Your board rankings and Elo ratings
  • Trait grades and scouting notes you create
  • Mock draft results and history

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. We don't sell your data, and we don't share it with third parties. If you use the site as a guest without signing in, nothing is saved — your session data lives in your browser and disappears when you leave.

Data Sources

Consensus rankings are aggregated from publicly available mock drafts and prospect evaluations across the draft community. Combine measurables come from official NFL and publicly reported sources. Team depth charts are sourced from ESPN. We do our best to keep everything accurate and up to date, but we're a small operation — if something looks off, let us know.
