privacy policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

What we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. That's it. We use this to identify your account and save your board data.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to power Big Board Lab — saving your rankings, trait grades, scouting notes, and mock draft history. We don't sell, share, or rent your personal information to anyone.

What we store

Your board data (rankings, traits, notes) is stored securely in our database hosted by Supabase. Community rankings are aggregated anonymously — other users can see averaged community consensus scores but never your individual board.

Cookies & local storage

We use browser local storage to cache your session and preferences for a faster experience. We don't use third-party tracking cookies or analytics.

Third-party services

We use Google OAuth for authentication and Supabase for data storage. Both services have their own privacy policies. We don't integrate with any advertising networks.

Data deletion

Want your data removed? Email lionofchoice15@proton.me and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll post the changes here with a new date. We won't make material changes without notice.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at lionofchoice15@proton.me.
